Our History

  • 2023
    Our Group is Founded
    Bytezo was established in 2023 on a JugendHackt Colone Event. We founded Bytezo, as a group of developers, to make software together.
  • Late 2023
    Start of Eventfully
    In 2023, we also started "MyEventWorld" that would later be renamed "AccessibilityEvents" and would finally be named Eventfully. We also applied for Youth-Hacking-For-Freedom (YH4F) Hackathon, with the goal to develop Eventfully further into a finished product.
  • January to June of 2024
    Development of Eventfully
    We started reworking Eventfully from the ground up. From January to June of 2024 we developed Eventfully, with weekly meetings, sharing our progress and bainstorming together.
  • September of 2024
    3rd Place at YH4F
    In August 2024, after an interview with YH4F, about Eventfully, we got the news that Eventfully won the 3rd place, winning the Awesome Hacker Award.
  • Future
    Future Innovation
    Looking ahead we want to found an Association, wanting to continue developing software.
